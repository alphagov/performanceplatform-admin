Apply for a dashboard

If you own or work on a transactional service on GOV.UK, you can now get a performance dashboard for it displaying the 4 digital KPIs (key performance indicators) for your service as required by the service standard.

You will need to send us basic details about you and your service and within 3 days we shall email you with a login to our Get a dashboard service.

The details about you and your service shall be reviewed by the Performance Platform team who shall the set up a GOV.UK Signon account for your dashboard and email you with login details within 3 working days.

About the 4 digital KPIs

The 4 digital KPIs are part of the Digital Service Design Manual.

They are:

  • Admin cost per user
  • Digital take-up
  • User satisfaction
  • Completion rate
